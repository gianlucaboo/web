HELLO!

I am a research fellow at WorldPop, University of Southampton, currently supporting population modeling and mapping in the GRID3 project. Previously, I earned a PhD in geography at the University of Zurich, where I led a research project at the intersection between geographic information systems, statistical modeling, and epidemiology. I also completed a postgraduate certificate in GIScience at the VU University Amsterdam and both an MSc and BSc in geography at the University of Lausanne. Parallel to my academic career, I worked as a geoinformatician for a Swiss regional office and as an urban planner/GIS manager for an NGO based in East Jerusalem.

My research interests lie in the area of spatial analysis, statistical modeling, and public health.
